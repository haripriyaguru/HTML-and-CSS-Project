Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. Alternatively, you can use the tracking number provided in your order confirmation email.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. The book must be in its original condition. Please contact our customer support team to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our services to other countries soon.

How can I become a seller on BookHaven?

If you're interested in selling books through our platform, please send an email to partners@bookhaven.com with details about your business.
