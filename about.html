About BookHaven

Your trusted partner in the world of books

Our Story

BookHaven was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make quality books accessible to everyone. What started as a small bookstore in Chennai has now grown into one of India's leading online bookstores.

Our journey began when our founder, Raj Kumar, a passionate book lover, realized the difficulty in finding diverse books in one place. He envisioned a bookstore that would house books from various languages, genres, and authors under one roof.

Today, BookHaven serves thousands of book lovers across India, delivering not just books but experiences and knowledge that transform lives.

Our Mission & Values

Promote Reading

We believe in the power of books to transform lives. Our mission is to promote reading culture across all age groups.
